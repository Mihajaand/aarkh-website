Structured Finance Transaction

At Aarkh Financial Services Ltd, we provide specialized legal services for structured finance transactions, offering innovative solutions to complex financing needs. Our team of experts works closely with clients to design and implement structured financing arrangements, ensuring that transactions are legally sound, tax-efficient, and aligned with business objectives. We assist in structuring, negotiating, and documenting a variety of financing transactions, helping clients achieve their financial goals while managing risk and compliance effectively.

We offer comprehensive services in securitization, including the creation of asset-backed securities (ABS) and the structuring of securitization transactions. Our team advises clients on structuring the underlying assets, the legal framework for issuing securities, and ensuring compliance with relevant regulations. We also assist in the preparation of offering documents, investor relations, and navigating the complexities of both public and private securitization transactions.

Our firm provides in-depth guidance on project finance and leveraged financing, advising on the structuring of complex, high-value financing arrangements. We help clients with the creation of financing structures for large-scale infrastructure, energy, and real estate projects, ensuring that these transactions are designed to mitigate risk and optimize capital efficiency. Additionally, we assist in structuring leveraged buyouts (LBOs) and debt financing arrangements, helping businesses access the capital necessary for growth and expansion.

We provide strategic advice on the use of derivatives and other financial instruments as part of structured finance transactions. Our expertise includes advising clients on interest rate swaps, currency hedges, and credit derivatives, assisting in the design of effective risk management strategies. We work closely with clients to ensure that these financial products are structured in a way that aligns with their overall financial objectives while complying with regulatory requirements.
