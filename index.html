Who are we?

  • Your Trusted Partner for Offshore Services and Compliance in Mauritius

    A&A Global Solutions (Mauritius) Ltd is a premier provider of offshore services in Mauritius, specializing notably in company and trust formation, coupled with comprehensive ongoing administration services. This encompasses the preparation of financial statements, provision of secretarial services, adherence to anti-money laundering and counter-financing of terrorism (AML/CFT) regulations, and ensuring legal compliance at every step.

  • Licensed Fiduciary and Corporate Services Provider

    We proudly hold a full fiduciary license granted by the Mauritius Financial Services Commission, authorizing the company to serve as a trusted fiduciary and corporate services provider.

  • Global Corporate and Fiduciary Solutions Tailored to Your Need

    With our worldwide network, we operate seamlessly across continents to deliver tailored corporate and fiduciary solutions that precisely align with client objectives. With structures ranging from domestic companies, global business or international companies, trust, foundations, closed end fund, collective investment schemes, protected cell companies, variable cell companies, insurance and pensions, we craft bespoke structures that embody a winning formula, carefully crafted to meet the unique needs of each client.

Frequently Asked Questions

A&A Global Solutions provides a wide range of services, including company and trust formation, fiduciary and corporate secretarial services, financial structuring, tax compliance, and adherence to AML/CFT regulations. The company also offers ongoing administration services such as maintaining financial records, preparing financial statements, and ensuring legal compliance.

Holding a full fiduciary license from the Mauritius Financial Services Commission authorizes A&A Global Solutions to act as a trusted provider of corporate and fiduciary services. This license ensures clients receive professional and compliant services, including trust management, company formation, and regulatory adherence.

A&A Global Solutions adheres to strict anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-financing of terrorism (CFT) regulations. They ensure all operations comply with domestic and international laws by maintaining proper financial records, conducting thorough due diligence, and providing professional oversight for all corporate and fiduciary activities.
